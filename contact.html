About Us

At Paws & Whiskers, we believe every furry friend deserves a forever home. 🏡💕 We’re a small, passionate team dedicated to connecting loving families with adorable, rescued animals. Whether it’s a playful pup, a gentle kitty, or a chirpy bird, our mission is to spread joy—one adoption at a time.

Thank you for being part of this journey! Feel free to reach out, share your thoughts, or ask us anything. We’re all ears (and tails)! 🐶🐱🐦
